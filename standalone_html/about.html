About partdro

We are the critical link in the unmanned aerial ecosystem, dedicated to keeping the world's drone fleets operational.

Our Story

Founded by experts in aerospace engineering and international logistics, partdro recognized a critical gap in the drone industry: the difficulty of sourcing reliable, original components quickly.

We set out to build a digital infrastructure that connects top-tier manufacturers directly with B2B clients, repair centers, and developers worldwide. Today, we are proud to be a trusted partner for those who rely on precision aerial technology.

Our Mission

To empower the drone industry by ensuring instant access to critical, high-quality components, minimizing downtime worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the central nervous system of the global drone supply chain, defining standards for speed, transparency, and quality.

Core Values

  • Precision: Accuracy in every part and shipment.
  • Integrity: Only genuine OEM components.
  • Speed: Keeping pace with the industry.
